About Me

I am Fatima Ahmad Aminu, a passionate, detail-oriented Computer Science undergraduate currently pursuing my Bachelor’s degree at Northwest University, Kano. As a 200-level student, I find myself deeply fascinated by how computers manage, render, and convey structured information to global audiences.

My passion for technology began with understanding how websites operate. I realized that behind every gorgeous interface is a highly structured foundation that communicates with browsers, search engines, and screen readers. This realization drove me to master HTML—the absolute core of the web—and to dedicate myself to becoming a professional Web Developer.

Throughout my academic journey, I have prioritized learning frameworks, semantic methodologies, and user accessibility. I believe that a great website must be usable by everyone, regardless of their device or capability.

When I am not debugging HTML pages or attending lectures at the university, you will most likely find me immersed in watching movies. This hobby allows me to study modern graphic layouts, pacing, and visual storytelling, which directly inspires how I design my web interfaces.

Academic Institution
